Blended Baked Oats

Making blended baked oats is SIMPLE!

The base recipe is comprised of only a few common household ingredients, and it is highly customizable to fit your flavor mood.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup oats
  • 1 banana
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • pinch of salt
  • Optional
    • blueberries
    • chocolate chips
    • whatever your heart desires!
