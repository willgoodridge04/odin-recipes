Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

The PBJ Sandwich: challenging but rewarding

Considered to be the most difficult meal to make by top food experts, the PBJ sandwich can be grueling to create.

Many fail in the endeavor, yet those whose succeed are left with an amazing dish to consume.

Ingredients (pay attention now)

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • Bread

Steps

And I cannont stress this enough

  1. Place two slices of bread shoulder-width apart on a level surface with a surface-friction high enough to prevent sliding of the bread during prep.
  2. GENTLY lather the peanut butter on one of the bread slices (preferably the left one) until uniformity is reached and a thickness of NO GREATER THAN 0.5cm is achieved.
  3. Give yourself a 5 minute breather, you're doing great! :)
  4. duplivate step 2 with precision, subbing jelly for the peanut butter.
  5. Slap those babbies together, and you got yourself a sammy!
