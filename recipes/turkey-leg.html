Well Hot-Shot, how does one go about making the Turkey Leg?

I'm glad you asked!

Ingredients

  • A leg or two from a turkey
  • 1 liter of water
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp hot sauce
  • 1 tbsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 tbsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp honey

How to Create Leg

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill
  2. In a large pot, combine all non-turkey ingredients and add legs
  3. Bring to a boil and cook 30-45 minutes until legs reach internal temp of 180 degrees
  4. Place legs on the preheated grill and cook, turning once, for around 20 minutes
